Writing Across Technology
The English Department at the University of Connecticut launched a new program called Writing Across Technology. The program focuses on teaching students about the different facets of writing and how it is not limited to just academic papers. The Design Center was asked to create a brand around this program and the five modes of meaning, hence the five-pointed asterisk to symbolize that there is something more to the program than meets the eye.
