KeyConnect

This project was an exploration in UI/UX and learning how to prototype using Figma.

During the start of the pandemic, the custom keyboard community grew as many were working from home and upgrading their workspace. As the community grew, people would build multiple keyboards and post online for others to view.

KeyConnect would serve as a social media platform aimed to keep track of a users keyboard build with all components and share for others to view and comment.
